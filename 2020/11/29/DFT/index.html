三角插值与DFT

Date: November 29, 2020  17:08:23

傅里叶级数

设是以为周期的平方可积函数,用三角多项式

做最佳平方逼近函数,由于三角函数族

在 上是正交函数族,于是在上的最佳平方三角逼近多项式的系数是

\frac{1}{2}a0+\sum^{\infty}{k=1}(a_k\cos kx+b_k\sin kx)

x_j=\frac{2\pi j}{2m+1},j =0,1,…,2m,

\sum^{2m}{j=0}\sin lx_j\sin kx_j=\left{\begin{matrix} 0,&l\neq k,l=k=0 \ \dfrac{2m+1}{2},&l=k\neq 0 \end{matrix}\right.\\sum^{2m}{j=0}\cos lxj\cos kx_j=\left{\begin{matrix} 0,&l\neq k \ \dfrac{2m+1}{2},&l=k\neq 0 \ 2m+1,&l=k=0\end{matrix}\right.\\sum^{2m}{j=0}\cos lx_j\sin kx_j=0,0\leq k,j\leq m

Sn(x)=1/2a_0+\sum{k=1}^n(a_k)\cos kx+b_k\sin kx),n<m

ak=\frac{2}{2m+1}\sum{j=0}^{2m}\cos \frac{2\pi jk}{2m+1},k=0,1,..,n,\bk=\frac{2}{2m+1}\sum{j=0}^{2m}\sin \frac{2\pi jk}{2m+1},k=0,1,..,n,

S_m(x_j)=f_j,j=0,1,…,2m,

Sm(x)=\frac{1}{2}a_0+\sum{k=1}{m}(a_k\cos kx+b_k\sin kx)

e^{ijx}=\cos (jx)+i\sin (jx),j=0,1,…,N-1,i = \sqrt(-1)

\phi_j = (1,e^{ij\frac{2\pi}{N}},…,e^{ij\frac{2\pi}{N-1}})^T

(\phil,\phi_s)=\sum{k=0}^{N-1}e^{il\frac{2\pi}{N}k}e^{-is\frac{2\pi}{N}k}=\sum_{k=0}^{N-1}e^{i(l-s)\frac{2\pi}{N}k}=\left{\begin{matrix} 0,&l\neq s \ N,&l=s\end{matrix}\right.\

S(x)=\sum_{k=0}^{n-1}c_ke^{ikx},n\leq N

ck=\frac{1}{N}\sum{j=0}^{N-1}f_je^{-ikj\frac{2\pi}{N}},k=0,1,..,n-1

n=NS(x_j)=f(x_j){f_j}{c_k}f(x)$$的离散傅里叶变换简称,DFT.
